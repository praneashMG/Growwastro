What is Tarot?

Tarot is a spiritual tool used for guidance, self-reflection, and decision-making. It consists of 78 cards divided into Major Arcana and Minor Arcana.

Each card carries symbolic meanings that help reveal hidden emotions, life patterns, and future possibilities. Tarot does not predict destiny — it provides insight and clarity.

Professional tarot readers interpret the cards based on intuition and card placement to deliver meaningful guidance.
